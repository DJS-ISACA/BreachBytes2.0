In this unique CTF challenge, participants must step into the shoes of master strategists tasked with uncovering a hidden plot to sabotage the greatest Clash of Clans war ever fought. A rogue clan has encrypted secret battle plans and hidden them across various in-game elements, social media profiles, and digital images of clan bases.

Only by mastering both open-source intelligence and the art of decoding concealed data will participants succeed in revealing the rogue clan’s strategy and save their kingdom from certain defeat. Welcome to Operation War Room: Clash of Secrets, where only the sharpest minds will break through the enemy’s digital defenses and win the ultimate war!
